The operator has a `portfolio.json` produced by `--transform` and wants domain-aware AI insights that explain consumption health in SAP-specific business terms at four levels of granularity: individual product contracts, L1 solution areas, individual customers, and industry verticals. This story delivers the `--analyze` tool: the operator runs `consumption-analyzer --analyze <portfolio.json>` and the tool executes a 4-step bottom-up AI pipeline that enriches the portfolio JSON in-place. Steps 1–3 use the sonnet model (AI_MODEL) and operate on contract data per L3 product, solution_architecture_insights per L1 solution area, and enterprise_architecture_insights per customer respectively; Step 4 uses the opus model (AI_MODEL_SENIOR) and produces industry-level cross-customer narratives. All prompt templates are `.md` files in `/src/ai/` with `{{placeholder}}` syntax; the sole product knowledge source is `src/ai/sap-product-catalog.json`. After each step completes, its output is written back to the portfolio JSON on disk so a crash loses at most one step.

A successful completion means the operator can run `--analyze` against a portfolio JSON and then open the file to find: `contract.contract_insights[]` populated for every L3 product (Step 1), `solutions_l1[].solution_architecture_insights[]` populated for every L1 solution area (Step 2), `customer.enterprise_architecture_insights[]` populated for every customer (Step 3), and `industry_insights[].summary[]` populated for every distinct industry (Step 4). The enriched portfolio JSON becomes the input to `--dashboard`, which renders all four levels of AI insight in the 3-pane dashboard without any additional processing.
