The operator has a `portfolio.json` produced by `--transform` and wants domain-aware AI insights that explain consumption health in SAP-specific business terms at five levels of granularity: individual product contracts, L2 solution groupings, L1 solution areas, individual customers, and industry verticals. This story delivers the `--analyze` tool: the operator runs `consumption-analyzer --analyze <portfolio.json>` and the tool executes a 5-step bottom-up AI pipeline that enriches the portfolio JSON in-place. Steps 1–3 use the sonnet model (AI_MODEL) and operate on contract data, L2 groupings, and L1 solution areas respectively; Steps 4–5 use the opus model (AI_MODEL_SENIOR) and produce customer-level account summaries and industry-level cross-customer narratives. All prompt templates are `.md` files in `/src/ai/` with `{{placeholder}}` syntax; the sole product knowledge source is `src/ai/sap-product-catalog.json`. After each step completes, its output is written back to the portfolio JSON on disk so a crash loses at most one step.

A successful completion means the operator can run `--analyze` against a portfolio JSON and then open the file to find: `contract.ai_insights[]` populated for every L3 product (Step 1), `solutions_l3[].solution_architecture_insights[]` populated for every L2 grouping (Step 2), `solutions_l1[].enterprise_architecture_insights[]` populated for every L1 solution area (Step 3), `customer.account_insights[]` populated for every customer (Step 4), and `industry_insights[].summary[]` populated for every distinct industry (Step 5). The enriched portfolio JSON becomes the input to `--dashboard`, which renders all five levels of AI insight in the 3-pane dashboard without any additional processing.
