The operator runs --transform against a cACV CSV that contains one or more customers. Before the portfolio JSON is written, the system needs to infer which industry vertical the customer (or customer set) belongs to — for example "Pharma/Life Sciences", "Manufacturing", or "Healthcare" — so that the dashboard and AI narrative can use industry-appropriate framing and benchmarks. This story implements that inference deterministically, without AI, using a combination of customer name heuristics and the product portfolio fingerprint (which SAP solution areas and sub-solution areas are active in the contract).

A successful completion means that every portfolio JSON produced by --transform contains a non-null industry field at the root level, derived purely from customer name patterns and LPR/solution-area presence rules encoded in a lookup table or ruleset. After this story is built, the dashboard STORY-005 can display the inferred industry as a context label, and the --analyze AI prompts STORY-003 can reference the industry vertical to produce more targeted architectural insights and recommendations without needing to re-derive it at analysis time.
